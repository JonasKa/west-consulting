About me and the company

Some History

Hi, I’m Jonas Karlsson, a Solution architect and developer from Gothenburg, Sweden.

I started West Consulting, or westc, in June 2017. My IT career started at WM-data and later on Logica (now called CGI) at 2005 and after 4 years I moved to Capgemini and stayed there for 8 years after starting my own business.

This means > 10 years of experience as a developer and Solution Architect. Go to the bottom of the page for contact information.

Characteristics

I enjoy both frontend and backend technologies and test driven development in a continuous delivery manner are fundamentals for me.

I prefer a role where I have time for coding but also to take responsibility in the overall solution, including tight communication with the customer, creating design proposals, giving estimates and even do the scrum master work.

Independently of what technology or what type of system I’m involved in building, I always have great customer focus on what should be delivered.
