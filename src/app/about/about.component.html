About the company

West IT Consulting AB was founded in June, 2017 by me, Jonas Karlsson. The idea behind the company is to provide IT consulting services. The base for the company is in Gothenburg, Sweden.

My career started at WM-data (Logica -> CGI) and after 4 years I moved to Capgemini and stayed there for 8 years. This means I have > 10 years of experience as a developer and Solution Architect. I enjoy booth frontend and backend technologies and test driven development in a continuous delivery manner are fundamentals for me.

I prefer a role where I have time for coding but also to take responsibility in the overall solution, including tight communication with the customer, creating design proposals, giving estimates and even do the scrum master work.

My main personal characteristics are my high ambition, loyalty and a pragmatic approach in designing solutions that are optimal for the customer.
